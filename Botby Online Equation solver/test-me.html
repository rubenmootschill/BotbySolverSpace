Tutorial?

History based
  1. First do some work in Solver, Quiz, Balance, or Premade Quiz so your history has equations in it.
  2. Open Test Me and it will load questions based on your recent or missed practice.
  3. Answer the multiple-choice questions and use the feedback to improve on weak areas.

Best for: review practice based on your own mistakes.
