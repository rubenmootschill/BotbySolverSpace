Project Story

Solver Space helps you understand equations—not just solve them.

Built by a teacher and a student, it turns algebra into step-by-step visual thinking.

How it started

"Students kept getting the right answer—but couldn't explain why."

Solver Space started in the classroom, where Benjamin (a special education teacher) noticed students following steps without understanding them. He partnered with Rolen to build a tool that shows the thinking, not just the result—so students could actually learn from every problem they solved.
