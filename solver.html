Supported Formats

This solver is built for student-friendly linear and quadratic equations.

✅ Supported

  • x + 3 = 10
  • 2x + 4 = 10
  • x² - 5x + 6 = 0

❌ Not supported yet

  • sin(x)
  • matrices
  • equations above x²

How This Solver Helps Students Learn

The solver does more than return an answer. It detects whether your equation is linear, quadratic, no-solution, or infinitely many solutions, then shows step-by-step reasoning.

For linear equations, the method is based on balancing both sides and isolating x. For quadratic equations, it uses either square-root logic or the quadratic formula depending on the structure.

Best practice: try the equation yourself first, then compare your steps with the solver output.
